What is there to say about me?


Plenty


Born and raised in Germany, I learned how to be an artist starting in Montessorri school, and unlike most parents I’ve heard about, mine were full on supportive of art being my center of the universe. Since I could remember, I’ve always been drawing from pencil and pad to computer and tablet.

Now, living in Miami, I work in the design field both in graphic design and hand made crochet clothing, I live a healthy lifestyle with a little indulgence to balance things out.
